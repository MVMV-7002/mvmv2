The Supra MK2(1981-1988)

Introduction: The second-generation Supra further separated itself from the Celica. It featured a more modern design and was powered by more advanced engines.

Engine: The A50 offered a variety of engines, including a 2.8L inline-six, a 2.0L turbo, and even a 2.8L turbocharged version in some markets.

Key Features: The styling became more angular and aggressive. It also marked the introduction of more technological features like an advanced suspension system.
